TITLE: Fund Transfer Between Accounts

USER STORY:
As a bank customer I want to transfer funds
between my accounts so that I can manage
my money efficiently.

ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA:
- Customer can transfer between own accounts only
- Transfer amount must be greater than zero
- Sufficient balance required before transfer
- Confirmation screen shown before final transfer
- Transaction receipt generated after success
- Transfer reflects immediately in both balances

TECHNICAL NOTES:
- POST /api/transactions/transfer endpoint
- Requires source account, target account, amount
- Idempotency key required to prevent duplicate transfers

DEFINITION OF DONE:
- Financial reconciliation tests passing
- Duplicate transfer prevention verified
- Balance update tests confirmed
